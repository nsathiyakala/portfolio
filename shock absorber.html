Shock Absorber
Energy Conservation In Suspension Due To Piezoelectric Effect
A new piezoelectric energy conservation device with high efficiency was designed for harvesting vibration energy from the suspension system during vehicle travel. By using this type of absorber we can absorb more number of shocks and variations are absorbed with the more Accuracy. This type of Suspension has no problem of leakage of oil like hydraulic shock absorber. Also this has less maintenance than other types of shock absorber that we can made this type of shock absorber for the efficient work of vehicle and for reducing the maintained cost of vehicle. The proposed model provides a powerful reference for future studies of energy harvesting from vehicle suspension system Overall, piezoelectric suspension systems have the potential to revolutionize the automotive industry and improve the driving experience for millions of people.
