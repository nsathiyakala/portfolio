Sign Language
A sign language glove is a wearable device that translates sign language gestures into spoken or written language. Equipped with sensors and technology, the glove detects the hand movements and positions used in sign language and converts them into corresponding words or sentences. This glove presents a promising solution for bridging communication gaps between individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing and those who are not familiar with sign language. By converting hand gestures into text displayed on an LCD screen, this technology has the potential to facilitate real-time communication and inclusivity. While there may be challenges in accuracy and user adaptation, ongoing advancements in gesture recognition and wearable technology offer opportunities for refinement. As this technology evolves, it could significantly enhance accessibility and foster better understanding between different communities.
